Welcome to The Good Sam!


Trials and tribulations can hit anytime anywhere. And in a Third World Country wherein calamities hit year over year, the Philippines and its countrymen need to support one another more than so, through the concept of BAYANIHAN. THE GOOD SAM looks to be the bridge between those in need and the Good Samaritans who can "give more, and be more".

THE GOOD SAM has a number of charities which we regularly support. We also have urgent initiatives for those in dire need like typhooon and fire victims, patients who cannot support themselves and the like. We do not limit ourselves as well with the current charities and initiatives we have, as people can get a hold of us and propose theirs s well. Read More of THE GOOD SAM below.

OUR SERVICES

Our Charities

These are the on-going charities of THE GOOD SAM which ranges from food distribution, medical care, and education. You can also be part of the benefactors by choosing which one you would like to support and donate.

Urgent Initiatives

Our Urgent Initiatives are situations which need urgent aid, or those that have a specific monetary goal or time limit. We found this to be a must as to mitigate sudden events such as typhoon and fire catastrophes or assist in crucial medical emergencies.

Enroll Your Charity

We want to spread the concept of "Bayanihan" to as much as we can. Those in need can also enroll their charities by filling up our form and we'll get back to you once we've finished our due diligence.

OUR CALLING

It was only by chance, Maan and I partnered together to start this up. Its our way of "paying it forward". We are not wealthy, but anybody can be a good samaritan, no matter what amount that may be. We hope that we can be a vessel to help others and truly inspire our Countrymen to be there for one another. They joy and fulfillment you get to give aid to the needy is truly something special.

Miguel Gutierrez
Co-founder at The Good Sam

We only spent a few minutes of talking to each other, before Miguel and I thought and agreed to do this. Business, money, and capital is important but in a very toxic culture and depressed environment right now, the World needs good news, good vibes, a good samaritan. Let us be the bridge for you to do so, or maybe even inspire you to do something just like this. We are not in competition with anyone, in fact we hope this replicates so that we may live in a better world.

Marie Lim
Co-founder at The Good Sam
