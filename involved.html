Our Charities


The Good Sam supports a number of charities and initiatives ranging from education, to health, and even vocational work. If you are interested in any of our charities, feel free to review them and donate any amount from your generous heart.

OUR LADY OF PENAFRANCIA SEMINARY

Support Seminarians in their journey to Priesthood

Father Roger and the seminarians.

Our Lady of Penafrancia Seminary, is located at Sorsogon City. Support our seminarist, in their education, school materials, and basic needs as they complete their journey to Priesthood.

MGA ANAK NI MARIA MAKILING

Provide laptops of students for blended learning

Turnover of laptops to Los Banos National Highschool.

Mga Anak ni Maria Makiling is a movement to provide laptops to the financially challenged students of Los Banos National Highschool, so that they may be able to cope with the changing times of technology and innovation.

Donate

PGH PEDIA WARD

Financial care for the cancer patients of PGH Pedia Ward

A Disney Princess show for the children during the Christmas Season

PGH Pedia Ward holds different cancer cases in children. This initiative looks to provide these patients support in terms of medical supplies, consult, and comfort for these patients so that they may be able to overcom the present challenge in their young lives.

MALABON FOUNDATION FOR THE POOR

Donate rice to the poor

Turnover of 500 Cavans of Rice to the foundation.

One of the initiatives of the Organization is to provide food to the needy and poor of Malabon City. Amount donated will be used to buy rice, canned goods, and water.
